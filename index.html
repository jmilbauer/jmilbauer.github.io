I am a PhD student in the Language Technologies Institute at Carnegie Mellon University's School of Computer Science, advised by Emma Strubell. My work investigates the epistemic role of artificial intelligence, with a focus on understanding and augmenting the way people discover and explore ideas.

Supported by the Mozilla.ai residency, I'm currently working on research agents.

Previously, I've worked at Google Research (now part of Google Deepmind), Knowledge Lab, Waymo (at Google X), and the Berkman-Klein Center. As an undergrad I studied computer science, philosophy, and linguistics at the University of Chicago. And before that I grew up in NYC.
