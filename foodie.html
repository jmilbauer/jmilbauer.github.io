Simmer - an app for foodies

There's a huge problem with the current state of food discovery platforms like Yelp and Google Maps -- they rate restaurants, not dishes. If I want the best burger in town, what can I do? Simmer solves this problem by using machine learning to extract dish-level preference information and allow users to search for great food at a more granular level. Check out the app!

Simmer (under the name "Foodie") was accepted to YCombinator Winter 2019, one of the world's top startup accelerators.

I left Simmer in Spring 2019 to focus on my research work.
